a little about me

Copy first. Chaos never.

I'm a Senior Copywriter with 6+ years turning briefs into copy that sounds like a person wrote it — because one did. I've built brand voices from scratch, led copywriting teams, and figured out how to make AI tools handle the repetitive parts so I can focus on the ones that actually need a human.

These days I write everything from Instagram captions to long-form guides for outlets — always chasing the version that's a little sharper than the last draft. Deadlines are non-negotiable, structure is a love language, and I still remember every brand guideline I've ever written.
